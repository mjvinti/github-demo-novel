Chapter 1
- We meet Nick, Tom, Daisy and Jordan
- Narrator Nick Carraway introduces himself, explaining his Midwestern roots, Yale education, and move to New York in 1922 to learn the bond business. He rents a modest home in West Egg—a Long Island community for the newly wealthy. He visits his cousin Daisy and her husband, Tom Buchanan, in neighboring East Egg, which is populated by the "old money" elite. There, Nick meets professional golfer Jordan Baker, and becomes aware of Tom’s infidelity. Returning home, Nick spots his mysterious neighbor, Jay Gatsby, standing on his lawn, gazing longingly at a green light across the water.
